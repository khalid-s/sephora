Sephora Saga

A unique concept

At the time Dominique Mandonnaud opened his first perfume in 1969, perfumes, creams and makeup were hidden behind counters, jealously guarded by intimidating sellers. The message was clear: "You are requested to buy before you try!"

Mandonnaud, revolutionizing the world of cosmetics deciding to put cosmetics in the heart of the store, available to all, giving customers the ability to move freely, to try the products, touch and feel.

A revolutionary idea

What bold idea! This is the key to success. Sephora model has revolutionized the sale of beauty products at retail. Today, through the doors of a Sephora store, you dive into a sensual, stimulating and fun world.

The sellers have been replaced by beauty advisors available to provide advice and recommendations. This place, where everyone can find the keys to his own beauty, offers a unique environment and experience teaching.

The Sephora brand S+ embodies the boldness, creativity and excellence in teaching.

These revolutionary products are both affordable and upscale, sensual and captivating. The Sephora brand was launched by the creation of 365 shades of lipstick - one for every mood, for every desire - and still products S+ make you want to have fun.

S+ is in a nutshell:

  • Top sales Sephora
  • One product sold every two minutes
  • 1400 references

In short, Sephora is:

  • 17,000 references
  • 6 million annual visitors in the Flagship of the Champs Elysees (more than visitors to the Eiffel Tower!)
  • Approximately 270 stores in France and 1700 in the World
  • 25,000 employees, including 4,000 in France

Subsidiary of the prestigious LVMH group since 1997, Sephora today has stores throughout Europe, but also in North America and more recently South America, the Middle East and Asia. And this development continues actively! Whether it is with our own brand S +, the head of sales and recognized for its quality and originality, or our website www.sephora.fr, reference site on cosmetics (our largest store with more than 13 million visitors per month) in all areas of innovation, we ensure that our competitors never catch us!

A Bright Future

Every day is an opportunity to challenge ourselves and improve ourselves, always conquering new skills and new markets. With our boldness, we are inspired by collaborations with designers, we develop exclusive partnerships with innovative labels, we invent store services - such as bars eyebrow or nail bars - and have a unique loyalty program .

Sephora again and still growing but the original idea remains the same. By offering our customers a "shopping experience" single - a place of discovery, sensuality, exchanges and expertise - Sephora promises an exciting time for everyone at all times.
