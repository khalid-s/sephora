PURPOSE OF THE LOYALTY PROGRAM

The SEPHORA loyalty program (hereinafter referred to as the "Loyalty Program") is a program introduced by Sephora Middle East FZE, a free zone company duly incorporated under the laws of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, having its registered offices at POBox 18536 , Dubai, United Arab Emirates and implemented in the United Arab Emirates, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Kingdom of Bahrain and State of Qatar through its local entities (hereinafter referred to as "Sephora").

This Loyalty Program allows customers holding a SEPHORA loyalty card to accumulate points on every purchase made at the Sephora Stores (as described hereinafter) entitling them to the benefits set forth herein.

The Loyalty Program has two levels:

  • The SEPHORA White card level (hereinafter referred to as the "White Card Program")
  • The SEPHORA Black card level (hereinafter referred to as the "Black Card Program")

SUBSCRIPTION TO THE LOYALTY PROGRAM

Eligibility for the Loyalty Program:

The Loyalty Program aims to provide benefits to a customer who has subscribed to the Loyalty Program and therefore owns a Sephora loyalty card allowing him/her to accumulate points, while purchasing products in the Sephora Stores, and to benefit from various offers as described herein (hereinafter referred to as the "Loyal Customer"). This fidelity card will be a SEPHORA White card in the White Card Program and a SEPHORA Black card in the Black Card Program (hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Loyalty Cards").

The Loyalty Cards are strictly reserved to the customers with a physical mailing address and/or postal address in the United Arab Emirates, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Kingdom of Bahrain and/or the State of Qatar (hereinafter referred to as the "Territory").

Each customer is only entitled to subscribe to one Loyalty Program (same family name, first name(s) and address) in the Territory. Therefore, Sephora reserves the right to request at any time any document to verify the identity of the customer wishing to subscribe to, or having subscribed to, a Loyalty Program especially in case a homonym already exists in Sephora’s data base.

Subscription to the Loyalty Program can be stored in Sephora’s data base and computer files only if the information disclosed by the customer on the White Card Program registration form is complete, readable and usable.

The Loyal Customer shall be liable to frequently update all relevant personal information he has communicated to Sephora during his/her subscription by asking to do so in-store, or by visiting the website loyalty.sephora-me.com. However, it is understood that Sephora cannot be held liable for the non receipt by a Loyal Customer of an offer due to outdated or false information.

Conditions for Obtaining the Sephora White Card:

The SEPHORA White card (hereinafter referred to as the "Sephora White Card") is freely available to anyone upon request when making a purchase in any of the Sephora stores in the United Arab Emirates,Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Kingdom of Bahrain, or State of Qatar (hereinafter referred to as the "Sephora Stores").

To activate the Sephora White Card, the Loyal Customer has to fill in a registration form at the cash till whereby all requested information will be automatically entered into the system by the cashier. Following registration, the newly joined Loyal Customer will only be able to earn any accumulated points after forty eight (48) hours have passed.

These conditions for obtaining the Sephora White Card can be amended by Sephora at any time and in Sephora’s absolute discretion.
